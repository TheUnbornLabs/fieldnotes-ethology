## Verified (checked 2026-07-02)

The following facts in Section 2 were checked against the live web and confirmed:

- Scientific name *Archilochus colubris*, family Trochilidae (order Apodiformes) — confirmed via Wikipedia and Cornell Birds of the World.
- IUCN Red List status: Least Concern — confirmed.
