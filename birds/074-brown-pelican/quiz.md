# Short Quiz

1. Brown pelicans hunt primarily by: a) Surface skimming  b) Plunge-diving from height  c) Cooperative underwater pursuit  d) Filter-feeding

2. The brown pelican wingspan is approximately: a) 1 meter  b) 1.5 meters  c) 2–2.5 meters  d) 4 meters

3. The primary cause of the brown pelican's mid-twentieth-century collapse was: a) Overhunting  b) Habitat loss  c) DDT bioaccumulation and eggshell thinning  d) Disease

4. The brown pelican's recovery is chiefly attributed to: a) The DDT ban and habitat protection  b) Hunting bans alone  c) Captive breeding alone  d) No real recovery occurred

5. Brown pelican chicks at hatching are: a) Precocial  b) Semi-precocial  c) Immediately independent  d) Altricial and helpless

6. The typical brown pelican clutch is: a) 1 egg  b) 2–3 eggs  c) 5 or more eggs  d) No egg

7. Brown pelicans are naturally distributed in: a) The Northern Hemisphere only  b) Africa and Asia  c) The coastal Americas  d) Worldwide

8. DDT harms pelican reproduction mainly by: a) Instantly killing adults  b) Thinning eggshells so eggs break during incubation  c) Blinding the birds  d) Preventing courtship

9. Pelicans avoid dive injury through: a) Diving very slowly  b) Diving only into deep water  c) Specialized skull, neck, and air-sac structures that absorb impact  d) Never fully submerging

10. Young pelicans develop diving skill mainly through: a) Instinct with no improvement  b) Observation and repeated practice  c) A single innate program fixed at hatching  d) No developmental change

**Answer Key:** 1-b, 2-c, 3-c, 4-a, 5-d, 6-b, 7-c, 8-b, 9-c, 10-b
