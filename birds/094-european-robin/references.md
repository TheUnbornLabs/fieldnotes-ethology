## 3. Verified (checked 2026-07-02)

The following facts were confirmed against live web sources on 2026-07-02:

- Scientific name *Erithacus rubecula*; family Muscicapidae (Old World flycatchers and chats) — formerly placed in Turdidae, now Muscicapidae — confirmed (Wikipedia; Britannica; Cornell Birds of the World).
- Not closely related to the American robin *Turdus migratorius* (a thrush) — confirmed (Wikipedia).
- Northern populations migratory, west/south sedentary — confirmed (Wikipedia).
- IUCN Red List status **Least Concern** — confirmed (widespread, common; Cornell/BirdLife).
