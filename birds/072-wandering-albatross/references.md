## Verified (checked 2026-07-02)

- IUCN Red List status: **Vulnerable** — confirmed (BirdLife International / IUCN Red List; Australian Museum). Book status correct.
- Wingspan: the largest reliably measured wandering albatross wingspan is ~3.63 m (a male taken by USNS *Eltanin* in the Tasman Sea, 18 Sept 1965); typical wingspan ~3.0–3.5 m. Sources: Wikipedia "Snowy albatross"; Australian Museum.
