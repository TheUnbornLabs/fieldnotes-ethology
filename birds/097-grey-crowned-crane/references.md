## 3. Verified (checked 2026-07-02)

The following facts were confirmed against live web sources on 2026-07-02:

- Scientific name *Balearica regulorum*; family Gruidae, order Gruiformes; sister/relative species is the black crowned crane *Balearica pavonina* — confirmed (Wikipedia; IUCN Crane Specialist Group).
- IUCN Red List status **Endangered** — uplisted from Vulnerable to Endangered in 2012; population declining (fastest-declining crane) — confirmed (BirdLife DataZone; Cambridge Bird Conservation International; savingcranes.org strategy).
- National bird of Uganda (on flag and coat of arms) — confirmed (Wikipedia).
