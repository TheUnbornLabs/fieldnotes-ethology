# Book 070 — The Emu: Quiz

1. Emus are monogamous, meaning: a) Males mate with multiple females  b) One male mates with one female across seasons  c) Females choose multiple males  d) No pair bonds form

2. Male emu parental care includes: a) No brooding; females do all care  b) Only courtship, no chick-rearing  c) Equal care with female  d) All brooding and chick-rearing

3. Emu chicks are: a) Altricial (helpless)  b) Semi-altricial  c) Raised in nests long-term  d) Precocial (mobile, foraging)

4. Emus are primarily: a) Territorial and site-faithful  b) Nomadic, following resources across vast areas  c) Migratory on set routes  d) Confined to small home ranges

5. Emu eggs are typically: a) White  b) Brown with speckles  c) Dark green  d) Blue-green

6. Primary predators of emu chicks are: a) Snakes only  b) Other emus  c) No natural predators  d) Dingoes and eagles

7. Emus communicate using: a) Only silent displays  b) Vocalizations and visual signals  c) Pheromones only  d) No communication

8. The "Great Emu War" of 1932 involved: a) Military culling attempt  b) Emus attacking humans  c) Mass starvation  d) Disease outbreak

9. Emu flocks: a) Have rigid hierarchies  b) Have fixed membership  c) Are dynamic with shifting membership  d) Never aggregate

10. Emus obtain water: a) Primarily from food; drink when available  b) Only from drinking  c) Cannot survive without water  d) Have no water needs

**Answer Key:** 1-b, 2-d, 3-d, 4-b, 5-c, 6-d, 7-b, 8-a, 9-c, 10-a
