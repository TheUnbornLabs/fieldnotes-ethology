## 3. Verified (checked 2026-07-02)

The following facts were confirmed against live web sources on 2026-07-02:

- Scientific name *Gyps fulvus*; family Accipitridae (hawks, eagles, Old World vultures), order Accipitriformes; one of the gregarious cliff-nesting *Gyps* vultures — confirmed (Wikipedia; Cornell Birds of the World; BirdLife DataZone).
- Obligate scavenger feeding almost entirely on carrion of medium-to-large mammals — confirmed (Cornell Birds of the World; Animalia.bio).
- IUCN Red List status **Least Concern** globally (Least Concern since 2004; population increasing/large range), masking severe regional threats especially poisoning — confirmed (IUCN Red List species/pdf/118593677; BirdLife DataZone; Cornell Birds of the World conservation account).
