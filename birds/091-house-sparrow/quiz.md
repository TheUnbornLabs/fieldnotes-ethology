# Book 091 — The House Sparrow: Quiz

1. The house sparrow belongs to which family?
   A. Passerellidae (New World sparrows)
   B. Passeridae (Old World sparrows)
   C. Fringillidae (finches)
   D. Corvidae (crows)

2. The house sparrow's global IUCN status is:
   A. Endangered
   B. Vulnerable
   C. Least Concern
   D. Critically Endangered

3. Nestling house sparrows are fed largely on:
   A. Seeds and grain only
   B. Fruit and nectar
   C. Regurgitated seeds only
   D. Insects and other invertebrates

4. The male house sparrow's black "badge" has been studied as a possible signal of:
   A. Age only
   B. Migratory readiness
   C. Dominance status
   D. Species identity to predators

5. Outside the breeding season, house sparrows typically:
   A. Live strictly solitary lives
   B. Migrate long distances individually
   C. Defend large exclusive territories year-round
   D. Form flocks and roost communally

6. Dust-bathing in house sparrows is best described as:
   A. A sign the bird cannot find water
   B. A normal comfort behavior linked to feather care and parasite control
   C. A courtship display only
   D. A response unique to introduced populations

7. House sparrows are described as socially monogamous, which means:
   A. They never mate outside the pair
   B. They form pair bonds but may still engage in extra-pair matings
   C. Only the female provides all care
   D. Pairs never last beyond one nesting attempt

8. The house sparrow's activity pattern is:
   A. Nocturnal
   B. Crepuscular only
   C. Diurnal
   D. Active only in winter

9. A major limiting resource shaping the house sparrow's breeding is:
   A. Suitable nest cavities and crevices
   B. Long migration routes
   C. Access to open water for diving
   D. Large hunting territories

10. Recent declines in some long-established urban house sparrow populations:
    A. Prove the species is going globally extinct
    B. Have a single, fully agreed cause
    C. Are real and locally steep, with debated, likely multiple causes
    D. Have not been observed anywhere

## Answer Key

1. B
2. C
3. D
4. C
5. D
6. B
7. B
8. C
9. A
10. C
