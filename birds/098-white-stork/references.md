## 3. Verified (checked 2026-07-02)

The following facts were confirmed against live web sources on 2026-07-02:

- Scientific name *Ciconia ciconia*; family Ciconiidae, order Ciconiiformes — confirmed (Wikipedia; Avibase).
- Migration: relies on thermal soaring; avoids long Mediterranean sea crossings by funnelling through the Strait of Gibraltar (west) and Bosphorus/Levant (east); winters largely in sub-Saharan Africa — confirmed (Wikipedia; primary study on Gibraltar crossings, PMC7708975). Eastern route carries ~530,000 storks annually.
- IUCN Red List status **Least Concern**, with recoveries aided by nest-platform provision — confirmed (Wikipedia; Cornell/BirdLife).
