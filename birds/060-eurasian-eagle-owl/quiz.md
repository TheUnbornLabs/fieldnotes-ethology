# Quiz — The Eurasian Eagle-Owl (*Bubo bubo*)

1. The Eurasian Eagle-Owl's "ear" tufts are:
   A. Its external ears
   B. Feathers used for display and camouflage
   C. Organs that echolocate
   D. Sound-collecting funnels for the ears

2. The eagle-owl's conservation status on the IUCN Red List is:
   A. Critically Endangered
   B. Endangered
   C. Vulnerable
   D. Least Concern

3. The eagle-owl's typical hunting style is best described as:
   A. Perch-and-ambush
   B. Continuous searching flight low over open ground
   C. Cooperative pack hunting
   D. Plunge-diving into water

4. Killing and eating other predators such as hawks and smaller owls is an example of:
   A. Intraguild predation
   B. Scavenging
   C. Kleptoparasitism
   D. Filter feeding

5. The deep "ooh-hu" hoot functions mainly as:
   A. A way to frighten prey before an attack
   B. An echolocation pulse
   C. A territorial and mate-communication signal
   D. A begging call by chicks

6. An eagle-owl's true ears are located:
   A. On the tips of the tufts
   B. Inside the beak
   C. As hidden openings at the sides of the head, behind the facial disc
   D. In the talons

7. Which best describes the eagle-owl's activity pattern?
   A. Strictly diurnal
   B. Active only at midday
   C. Continuously active day and night
   D. Mainly nocturnal, with crepuscular peaks

8. Eagle-owls most often nest:
   A. In woven hanging nests
   B. In deep underground burrows they dig
   C. On cliff or rock ledges, scrapes, or borrowed nests, with little or no construction
   D. In floating nests on water

9. The staggered ages of eagle-owl chicks in a brood result from:
   A. Eggs hatching all at once
   B. Incubation beginning with the first egg laid
   C. Chicks being born in different years
   D. The male laying some of the eggs

10. A major reason the eagle-owl has recovered in Europe is:
    A. It stopped needing to eat
    B. Protection and reintroduction, plus its readiness to use quarries and man-made sites
    C. It became fully diurnal
    D. It migrated permanently out of Europe

## Answer Key

1. B
2. D
3. A
4. A
5. C
6. C
7. D
8. C
9. B
10. B
