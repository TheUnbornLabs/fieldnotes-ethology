## Verified (checked 2026-07-02)

- Scientific name *Bubo bubo*, order Strigiformes, family **Strigidae** (typical owls), genus *Bubo* — confirmed (BirdLife DataZone; Animal Diversity Web; IUCN Red List).
- IUCN Red List status **Least Concern** — confirmed (IUCN Red List, species 166223956; BirdLife DataZone). Very large range (~32 million km²); population 250,000–2.5 million. Note: some sources describe the overall trend as slowly declining despite the LC status and European recovery; the book's "recovering or stable population in Europe following historical declines" is accurate for the European context.
