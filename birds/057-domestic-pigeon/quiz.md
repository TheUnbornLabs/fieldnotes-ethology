# Quiz — Book 057: The Pigeon

1. What is the scientific name of the rock/domestic pigeon?
   A) *Zenaida macroura*
   B) *Columba livia*
   C) *Corvus corax*
   D) *Columbina passerina*

2. A "time-compensated sun compass" means the pigeon:
   A) Corrects the sun's position for time of day using an internal clock
   B) Only navigates at noon
   C) Ignores the sun entirely
   D) Uses the moon instead of the sun

3. Which two mechanisms are debated as the basis of pigeon magnetoreception?
   A) Cones and rods
   B) Infrasound and ultrasound
   C) Taste and touch
   D) Iron-mineral (magnetite) receptors and light-dependent cryptochrome

4. The olfactory-navigation hypothesis is especially associated with:
   A) B. F. Skinner
   B) Floriano Papi
   C) Charles Darwin
   D) Shigeru Watanabe

5. Crop milk is:
   A) Milk stolen from mammals
   B) A crop pest
   C) A nutrient-rich secretion both parents feed to squabs
   D) A type of seed

6. A young nestling pigeon is called a:
   A) Squab
   B) Kit
   C) Cygnet
   D) Poult

7. In a famous cognition study associated with Watanabe, pigeons learned to:
   A) Speak words
   B) Build tools
   C) Migrate thousands of miles
   D) Discriminate paintings by different artists

8. Pigeons were central to which scientist's operant-conditioning research?
   A) Konrad Lorenz
   B) Jane Goodall
   C) B. F. Skinner
   D) Niko Tinbergen

9. Feral city pigeons are best described as:
   A) A wild native species
   B) Escaped mourning doves
   C) Descendants of domesticated rock doves
   D) A recently evolved new species

10. Flocking helps pigeons avoid predators mainly by:
    A) Making them invisible
    B) Poisoning attackers
    C) Increasing vigilance and confusing predators with many moving targets
    D) Digging burrows

## Answer Key

1-B, 2-A, 3-D, 4-B, 5-C, 6-A, 7-D, 8-C, 9-C, 10-C
