## Verified (checked 2026-07-02)

- Mass and size: ~2.5–4.1 kg; wingspan ~147–180 cm; the bulkiest of the grey geese; wild ancestor of most domestic geese. Sources: Wikipedia; Animal Diversity Web. Book's figures ("2.5 to 4 kg," "1.5 to 1.8 metres," ancestor of domestic geese) verified.

- Scientific name *Anser anser*; family Anatidae, order Anseriformes; Konrad Lorenz's classic study species for imprinting: verified.
