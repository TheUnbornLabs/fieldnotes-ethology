## 3. Verified (checked 2026-07-02)

The following facts were confirmed against live web sources on 2026-07-02:

- Scientific name *Hirundo rustica*; family Hirundinidae (swallows and martins) — confirmed (Wikipedia; Planet of Birds; Animal Diversity Web).
- Most widespread swallow / one of the largest breeding ranges of any passerine; near-global distribution — confirmed (Wikipedia; JungleDragon).
- IUCN Red List status **Least Concern** (range ~51.7 million km2, ~190 million individuals) — confirmed (Wikipedia; IUCN). Note: regional declines recognised (e.g. Canada COSEWIC).
