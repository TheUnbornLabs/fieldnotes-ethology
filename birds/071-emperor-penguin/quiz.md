# Short Quiz

1. Emperor penguins breed: a) In summer  b) In Antarctic winter  c) Year-round  d) Only in leap years

2. During incubation, the male emperor penguin: a) Shares the egg with the female  b) Builds a nest of sticks  c) Incubates alone for months while fasting  d) Digs a burrow

3. The emperor penguin pair-bond is best described as: a) Monogamous within a season, with moderate fidelity between years  b) Strictly lifelong with no mate switching  c) Polygamous  d) Non-existent

4. Emperor penguin chicks at hatching are: a) Precocial and independent  b) Altricial and helpless  c) Able to swim immediately  d) Fully feathered

5. Exceptional emperor penguin dives can exceed: a) 50 meters  b) 200 meters  c) 500 meters  d) They do not dive

6. Emperor penguins recognize mates and chicks mainly through: a) Individual vocalizations  b) Visual color patterns  c) Scent  d) Magnetic sense

7. The principal modern threat to emperor penguins is: a) Overhunting  b) Sea ice loss driven by climate change  c) Oil pollution  d) Introduced predators

8. Emperor penguin chicks aggregate in: a) Underground burrows  b) Stick nests  c) Individual territories  d) Crèches for warmth and protection

9. A typical emperor penguin clutch is: a) 2–3 eggs  b) 5 or more eggs  c) 1 egg  d) No egg (live birth)

10. Emperor penguins breed almost exclusively on: a) Sub-Antarctic temperate islands  b) Tropical coasts  c) Northern ocean shores  d) Antarctic fast ice

**Answer Key:** 1-b, 2-c, 3-a, 4-b, 5-c, 6-a, 7-b, 8-d, 9-c, 10-d
