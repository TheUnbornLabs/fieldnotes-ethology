# Book 069 — The Ostrich: Quiz

1. Ostriches are the world's: a) Fastest birds  b) Largest and heaviest living birds  c) Most common birds  d) Best fliers

2. Ostrich eggs weigh approximately: a) 100 grams  b) 500 grams  c) 1–2 kilograms  d) 5 kilograms

3. Ostrich chicks are: a) Altricial (helpless)  b) Semi-altricial  c) Fed by parents long-term  d) Precocial (mobile, foraging independently)

4. Multiple females in an ostrich territory: a) Form a hierarchy  b) Are rivals only  c) Never interact  d) Share a communal nest

5. Ostrich incubation is primarily done by: a) Females only  b) Males only  c) Males at night, females during day  d) Both equally all the time

6. Ostriches' running speed is approximately: a) 30 km/h  b) 50+ km/h sustained  c) 100+ km/h always  d) They cannot run fast

7. Ostrich territorial males display through: a) Song only  b) Silent combat  c) Nest-building displays  d) Deep vocalizations and visual displays

8. The "ostrich burying head" myth results from: a) Actual burying  b) Lowering head to tend eggs  c) Panic response  d) Cowardice

9. Ostriches defend against predators by: a) Running fast and kicking  b) Flying away  c) Hiding  d) Playing dead

10. Ostrich farming developed primarily for: a) Feathers, then meat and leather  b) Meat only  c) Eggs  d) Skin only

**Answer Key:** 1-b, 2-c, 3-d, 4-d, 5-c, 6-b, 7-d, 8-b, 9-a, 10-a
