# Book 069 — The Ostrich: Glossary

**Communal nesting** — multiple females laying eggs in the same nest.

**Crèche** — a group of chicks from multiple families cared for together.

**Flightless** — unable to fly due to reduction of flight musculature and skeletal modifications.

**Incubation** — brooding eggs to maintain temperature and humidity until hatching.

**Nomadic** — moving from place to place in search of food and water.

**Polygamy** — a mating system where one individual mates with multiple partners.

**Precocial** — of young birds, mobile and able to forage independently at hatching.

**Ratite** — a member of a group of large, flightless birds (ostriches, emus, rheas, kiwis).

**Territorial** — defending an area against rivals and intruders.

**Vigilance** — watchfulness for predators and threats.
