# Quiz — The Mourning Dove (*Zenaida macroura*)

1. To which family does the Mourning Dove belong?
   A. Corvidae
   B. Columbidae
   C. Strigidae
   D. Fringillidae

2. The Mourning Dove's soft, mournful *coo-OO-oo-oo-oo* is:
   A. The call of an owl
   B. A distress cry
   C. The male's perch-coo advertising song
   D. A mechanical wing sound

3. The whistling sound heard when a dove takes off is produced by:
   A. Air passing over modified flight feathers
   B. The voice box (syrinx)
   C. The bill snapping
   D. The feet

4. "Crop milk" fed to dove squabs is:
   A. Produced only by the female
   B. Actual mammalian milk
   C. A secretion from the crop lining, produced by both parents
   D. Regurgitated insects

5. The Mourning Dove's diet consists mainly of:
   A. Seeds and grain
   B. Insects
   C. Fish
   D. Fruit and nectar

6. Doves swallow grit (gastroliths) in order to:
   A. Attract mates
   B. Aid flight
   C. Produce crop milk
   D. Grind hard seeds in the gizzard

7. A distinctive feature of how columbids drink is that they:
   A. Do not drink at all
   B. Absorb water through the skin
   C. Only drink dew
   D. Draw water up by suction without tilting the head back

8. A typical Mourning Dove clutch contains:
   A. One egg
   B. Two eggs
   C. Five to six eggs
   D. Ten eggs

9. Which statement about Mourning Dove behavior is accurate?
   A. They are strictly non-territorial pacifists
   B. They never form flocks
   C. Males defend breeding territories and can fight
   D. Only females feed the young

10. The Mourning Dove remains abundant despite being heavily hunted mainly because of its:
    A. Venom
    B. Large body size
    C. High reproductive output and multiple broods
    D. Nocturnal habits

## Answer Key

1-B, 2-C, 3-A, 4-C, 5-A, 6-D, 7-D, 8-B, 9-C, 10-C
