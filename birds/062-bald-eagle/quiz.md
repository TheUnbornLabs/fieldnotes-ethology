# Quiz — The Bald Eagle

1. To which group does the bald eagle belong?
   A. Sea- and fish-eagles (*Haliaeetus*)
   B. Booted eagles (*Aquila*)
   C. Falcons
   D. Ospreys

2. The word "bald" in "bald eagle" refers to:
   A. A featherless head
   B. An old word meaning white-headed
   C. Its bald patches in molt
   D. Its bare legs

3. Kleptoparasitism in the bald eagle means:
   A. Catching only live fish
   B. Nesting in another bird's nest
   C. Stealing food caught by other animals
   D. Feeding its young by regurgitation

4. The screaming cry attributed to bald eagles in films is usually dubbed from:
   A. A golden eagle
   B. An osprey
   C. A peregrine falcon
   D. A red-tailed hawk

5. The cartwheel display is part of the bald eagle's:
   A. Feeding behavior
   B. Courtship behavior
   C. Migration
   D. Nest defense against predators

6. Rough toe projections that help eagles grip slippery fish are called:
   A. Spicules
   B. Spurs
   C. Talons
   D. Ceres

7. Adult bald eagle plumage (white head and tail) develops at about:
   A. One year
   B. Two years
   C. Five years
   D. Ten years

8. DDT harmed bald eagles mainly by:
   A. Poisoning them outright in flight
   B. Destroying their nesting trees
   C. Reducing fish populations directly
   D. Thinning their eggshells through biomagnification

9. The bald eagle was removed from the U.S. endangered/threatened list in:
   A. 1972
   B. 1985
   C. 2007
   D. 2015

10. Compared with the golden eagle, the bald eagle is:
    A. More tied to water and more of a scavenger and pirate
    B. Strictly a mammal hunter of open country
    C. Smaller and non-migratory everywhere
    D. Unable to catch live prey

## Answer Key

1. A
2. B
3. C
4. D
5. B
6. A
7. C
8. D
9. C
10. A
