# Book 068 — The Wild Turkey: Glossary

**Alloparenting** — care of young by individuals other than the parents.

**Clutch** — the set of eggs laid in one breeding bout, typically 8–15 for turkeys.

**Display** — a stereotyped behavior used in communication.

**Gobble** — the loud, resonant call of a male turkey.

**Gobbler** — an adult male turkey.

**Hen** — an adult female turkey.

**Jake** — a young male turkey in its first year.

**Poult** — a young turkey; chick.

**Precocial** — of young birds, mobile and relatively independent at hatching.

**Roost** — to rest or sleep, especially in trees for safety from predators.

**Strut** — a group of male turkeys associated together; also the stiff-legged walking display.

**Vigilance** — watchfulness for predators and threats.
