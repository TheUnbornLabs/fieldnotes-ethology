# Quiz — The Budgerigar

1. What is the natural (wild) coloration of the budgerigar?
   A. Blue and white
   B. Green and yellow
   C. Grey and violet
   D. Pure yellow

2. The budgerigar's movement pattern in the wild is best described as:
   A. Fixed year-round territories
   B. Regular seasonal migration on set routes
   C. Nomadic and irruptive, tracking rainfall
   D. Entirely sedentary

3. "Open-ended vocal learning" means the budgerigar:
   A. Learns its calls only as a chick, then fixes them
   B. Can modify its vocalisations throughout life
   C. Cannot learn new sounds at all
   D. Only mimics human speech

4. Call convergence in budgerigars refers to:
   A. Calls getting louder in large flocks
   B. Mates and flockmates coming to share call types through learning
   C. Genetically identical calls in all budgerigars
   D. Calls fading as birds age

5. Budgerigars are historically notable in yawning research because they were the first:
   A. Bird ever filmed yawning
   B. Non-mammal shown to exhibit contagious yawning
   C. Animal shown to yawn at all
   D. Parrot to stop yawning in captivity

6. In mate-choice experiments, female budgerigars were shown to prefer males that:
   A. Sang the loudest
   B. Had the bluest plumage
   C. Could solve a puzzle box (demonstrated problem-solving)
   D. Were the largest

7. Budgerigar colour vision is:
   A. Monochromatic
   B. Dichromatic
   C. Trichromatic like humans
   D. Tetrachromatic, including ultraviolet sensitivity

8. Wild budgerigar breeding is best described as:
   A. Fixed to a single spring season each year
   B. Opportunistic, triggered by rainfall and seed flush
   C. Continuous year-round regardless of conditions
   D. Occurring only in drought

9. The budgerigar's IUCN Red List status is:
   A. Critically Endangered
   B. Endangered
   C. Vulnerable
   D. Least Concern

10. Compared with the African grey parrot, the budgerigar is:
    A. Larger and longer-lived
    B. A small, nomadic, flock-living seed-eater
    C. A solitary forest specialist
    D. Non-vocal

## Answer Key

1. B
2. C
3. B
4. B
5. B
6. C
7. D
8. B
9. D
10. B
