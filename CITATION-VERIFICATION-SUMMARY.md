# Citation & Fact Verification Summary

**Completed:** July 2, 2026
**Scope:** All 100 books. For each, the core identity facts (scientific name, taxonomic placement, current IUCN Red List status) and the most prominent quantitative/behavioural claims were checked against live authoritative sources — IUCN Red List / BirdLife DataZone, Cornell Lab Birds of the World & All About Birds, Animal Diversity Web, The Reptile Database, AmphibiaWeb, NOAA Fisheries, US FWS, CITES, and museum/university pages. Every `references.md` now carries a dated **"Verified (checked 2026-07-02)"** block naming the confirming source for each fact.
