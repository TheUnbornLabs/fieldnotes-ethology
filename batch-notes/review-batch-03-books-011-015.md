Reviewer: Senior Ethology Reviewer / Scientific Editor
Verification level: LIGHT (no web search; relies on well-established biological knowledge). Uncertain specific claims are flagged here and, where appropriate, in the documents.

## 1. Batch Reviewed

- 011 — Spotted Hyena (*Crocuta crocuta*)
- 012 — African Wild Dog (*Lycaon pictus*)
- 013 — Brown Bear (*Ursus arctos*)
- 014 — Red Fox (*Vulpes vulpes*)

Each book's five files (book.md, summary.md, glossary.md, quiz.md, references.md) were read in full and checked for ethological accuracy, anthropomorphism control, anti-AI-slop, scientific honesty, structure/publishing conformance, quiz correctness, glossary quality, summary quality, and reference integrity, against the Book 001 (Wolf) benchmark.

Overall finding: this is a strong, mature batch. All five books are ethologically accurate on their core social systems, control anthropomorphism well, are free of AI-slop filler and moral fables, and treat debated claims with appropriate caution. References are correctly separated into "well-established" and "to verify," with no fabricated citations. Only one substantive editorial defect was found and corrected (Book 015 quiz answer key).

## 2. Major Corrections Made

- **Book 015 (Domestic Dog) — quiz answer key was "B" for all 10 questions.** Every answer was factually correct, but a uniform all-B key is a genuine quiz-design defect: it is trivially gameable and reads as an error. Corrected by re-ordering the option lists for questions 1, 3, 6, 8, and 10 (content unchanged) so the correct answer is distributed across positions. New key: 1-A, 2-B, 3-C, 4-B, 5-B, 6-C, 7-B, 8-A, 9-B, 10-D. Edits were applied identically to both `quiz.md` and Section 19 of `book.md` so the two remain synchronized. All answers remain correct against the book text.

No other major corrections were required. No ethological errors, no species/folder/number mismatches, no missing sections, no placeholders, and no fabricated citations were found in any of the five books.

## 3. Minor Corrections Made

- None beyond the Book 015 quiz fix. The other four books required no edits. (A few stylistic observations that did NOT warrant editing are noted below under Publication Readiness reasons — e.g. Book 013 uses a bulleted Section 2 rather than the benchmark's prose, which is an acceptable in-house variation, not an error.)
