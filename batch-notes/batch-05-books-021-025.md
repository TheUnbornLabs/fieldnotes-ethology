# Batch 05 — Books 021–025

**Theme:** Underestimated minds. A livestock flocker (sheep), two lab-model rodents whose natural behavior is richer than assumed (rat, mouse), a social prey specialist (rabbit), and a cultural marine mind (bottlenose dolphin). The batch repeatedly contrasts folk assumptions with cognitive and social evidence.

## Notable myth corrections

Sheep as mindless followers; rats as the sole cause of plague and as empathy-less; mice loving cheese and being silent reflex machines; rabbits as low-maintenance carrot-eaters; dolphins as uniformly altruistic and possessing ESP.
