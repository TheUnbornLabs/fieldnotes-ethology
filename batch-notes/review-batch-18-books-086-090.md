## 2. Major Corrections Made (all authored from stub)

Because the books were empty stubs, "corrections" here means the substantive scientific-accuracy decisions made during authoring, and the anti-anthropomorphism / cautious-framing choices applied per the benchmark:

- 086 Toco Toucan — Bill treated as multi-function (feeding reach + visual/social signalling + thermoregulatory radiator), NOT single-purpose. Thermoregulation-radiator role attributed carefully at topic level to Glenn Tattersall and colleagues without inventing a study-level citation. Explicitly corrected the popular "heavy bill" and "fearsome weapon" misconceptions (lightweight keratin-over-air-strut construction). Nest-hole nesting stated as use of existing cavities (cannot excavate) — placed correctly against its Piciformes/woodpecker relationship. Nest-predation (opportunistic omnivory) included to counter the "gentle fruit-eater" image.
- 087 Satin Bowerbird — Central correction enforced throughout: the bower is NOT a nest (Sections 1, 9, 14). Male provides no parental care; female-only care. Sexual selection by female choice made the organising theme. Male colour maturation (delayed plumage maturation; green immature males) handled, including the myth that all green birds are female. Bower-building framed as learned/experience-dependent, not "instinct-only." Aesthetics/forced-perspective discussed but NOT asserted as human-like art.
- 088 Superb Lyrebird — Vocal mimicry presented with the required caution: the viral "chainsaw / camera-shutter" clips flagged as largely captive-/human-habituation-derived and NOT typical of wild repertoire (Sections 8, 13, 14). Mimicry framed as learned (vocal learning / cultural transmission), not innate. Polygyny with female-only care and single-egg clutch stated. Vocal genius explicitly not generalised to overall intelligence.
- 089 Village Weaver — Male builds the woven nest; female inspects and accepts/rejects (female choice on a constructed object). Colonial polygyny stated. Nest-building framed as strong innate basis PLUS learning/experience (explicit rejection of "instinct-only," per instruction). Egg recognition / brood-parasite defence included. Pest status acknowledged.
- 090 European Starling — Murmuration presented as self-organised collective behaviour / emergence from local rules with NO leader and NO "group mind" (Sections 7, 11, 14, 16) — the single most important framing for this species. Invasive spread from the 1890s New York introduction covered, with the "Shakespeare/American Acclimatization Society" account flagged as debated rather than asserted. Native-range decline vs introduced-range superabundance paradox made explicit against the global Least Concern status. Commensalism and open-bill probing included.
