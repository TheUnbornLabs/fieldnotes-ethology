## Weak sections to revisit

Learning/Intelligence sections are the softest across this batch (feline cognition benchmarks, cattle "eureka" finding, pig joystick attribution, goat memory-retention interval) — all deliberately hedged and dependent on studies to confirm. Domestication dates stated as approximate ranges.

## Notable myth corrections

Cats aloof/purr-only-means-happy; bulls enraged by red (they are dichromatic, reacting to movement); pigs dirty/sweaty/"teacup"; goats eat tin cans / "faint" from fear (myotonia congenita); horses need to be "dominated" and are colorblind.
