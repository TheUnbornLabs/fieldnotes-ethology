## Verified (checked 2026-07-02)

- Scientific name *Danaus plexippus* and classification (Lepidoptera; Nymphalidae; Danainae) — confirmed.
- **IUCN Red List status of the migratory monarch (*Danaus plexippus plexippus*): Vulnerable.** First assessed Endangered in July 2022, reassessed to Vulnerable effective December 2023 (the change reflected corrected assessment data, not a population recovery). Sources: IUCN press release (iucn.org/press-release/202207/migratory-monarch-butterfly-now-endangered-iucn-red-list); IUCN Red List Standards and Petitions Ruling (Sept 2023); Monarch Joint Venture (monarchjointventure.org).
