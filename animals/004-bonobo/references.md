# References / Further Reading — The Bonobo (*Pan paniscus*)

## Well-established sources (safe to name)

- **IUCN Red List — *Pan paniscus* (bonobo)** — official species assessment for conservation status (Endangered), geographic range, population trend, and threats.
- **Frans de Waal, *Bonobo: The Forgotten Ape*** — widely cited accessible synthesis of bonobo behavior, including socio-sexual behavior, reconciliation, and social tolerance.
- **Takayoshi Kano, *The Last Ape: Pygmy Chimpanzee Behavior and Ecology*** — foundational account of long-term field research at Wamba.
- **Brian Hare and colleagues** — the general body of published work developing the bonobo self-domestication hypothesis. Name the framework and its lead proponents; verify specific individual papers before citing them by title, year, or journal.
- **Jane Goodall, *The Chimpanzees of Gombe*** — standard reference for chimpanzee behavior, useful for the comparative (Section 15) material.

## Verified (checked 2026-07-02)

The following were checked against the live web on 2026-07-02 and confirmed:
