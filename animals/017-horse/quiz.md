# Short Quiz — The Horse (*Equus caballus*)

1. The horse is best described as:
   A. A predator built to hunt in packs
   B. A grazing prey species built to detect danger and flee
   C. A solitary ambush hunter
   D. An omnivore that scavenges

2. The horse's roughly 350-degree field of view comes with blind spots:
   A. Only on the left side
   B. Directly in front of the nose and directly behind the tail
   C. Above and below the head
   D. There are no blind spots

3. Horse color vision is:
   A. Full trichromatic color like humans
   B. Completely colorblind (only grey)
   C. Dichromatic, seeing mainly blues and yellows
   D. Based on ultraviolet only

4. The natural social unit of horses is:
   A. A solitary individual
   B. A harem-type band of mares and young with a stallion, plus bachelor bands
   C. A rigidly ranked pack led by a permanent alpha
   D. A pair bond for life

5. In horse societies, dominance and leadership are:
   A. Exactly the same thing
   B. Different: dominance is yielding over resources, leadership is influence over movement and can be shared
   C. Both fixed permanently in one "lead mare"
   D. Absent entirely

6. The horse's primary defense against predators is:
   A. Fighting with horns
   B. Camouflage and freezing for hours
   C. Vigilance and rapid flight
   D. Venom

7. As a hindgut fermenter, the horse naturally needs to:
   A. Eat two large meals a day
   B. Fast for long periods
   C. Graze more or less continuously in many small meals
   D. Eat mainly meat

8. Ears pinned tightly flat against the neck with a tense face most likely signal:
   A. Always simple curiosity
   B. A threat, when read together with the rest of the body
   C. That the horse is asleep
   D. Hunger only

9. EquiFACS is:
   A. A horse breed
   B. A feeding schedule
   C. A system for coding horse facial muscle movements, useful for welfare
   D. A type of bit

10. Compared with the horse, the plains zebra:
   A. Was domesticated more successfully
   B. Shares a similar band structure but was never truly domesticated, largely due to temperament and reactivity
   C. Is a predator
   D. Lacks any social structure

## Answer Key

1. B
2. B
3. C
4. B
5. B
6. C
7. C
8. B
9. C
10. B
