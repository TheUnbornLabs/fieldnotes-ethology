# Short Quiz — Ants (family Formicidae)

1. The term "superorganism" applied to an ant colony means:
   A. The colony functions as a single biological unit, with queen-as-germline and workers-as-body
   B. The colony has one giant ant that controls the others
   C. Every ant is unusually large
   D. The colony can survive without any reproduction

2. Ants find the shortest route to food mainly because:
   A. Scouts measure and compare distances
   B. Shorter routes are reinforced with pheromone sooner, amplifying in a feedback loop
   C. The queen directs traffic
   D. Ants can see the whole path from above

3. Stigmergy refers to:
   A. A type of sting
   B. Coordination through changes made to the environment
   C. Mouth-to-mouth food sharing
   D. The mating flight

4. Leafcutter ants use the leaves they cut to:
   A. Eat directly as their main food
   B. Build waterproof walls
   C. Grow a fungus that they eat
   D. Line the nuptial-flight chamber

5. Cuticular hydrocarbons are important because they:
   A. Power the ant's flight muscles
   B. Digest seeds
   C. Are the main trail pheromone
   D. Carry colony identity and allow nestmate recognition

6. Which statement about ant activity is correct?
   A. Every worker labours nonstop
   B. Only the queen ever works
   C. A large fraction of workers are inactive reserves at any time
   D. Ants never rest

7. Trophallaxis is:
   A. A defensive acid spray
   B. A worker caste
   C. The counting of steps during navigation
   D. Sharing of liquid food mouth-to-mouth among colony members

8. Compared with honeybees, ants mainly communicate the location of food by:
   A. A symbolic waggle dance
   B. Laying pheromone trails in the environment
   C. Ultraviolet colour signals
   D. Loud sounds

9. Haplodiploidy in ants means:
   A. Colonies have two queens
   B. Females come from fertilised (diploid) eggs and males from unfertilised (haploid) eggs
   C. Ants have half a brain
   D. Workers can reproduce freely

10. An army ant bivouac is:
    A. A permanent underground fortress
    B. A fungus garden
    C. A seed granary
    D. A temporary shelter formed from the workers' own interlinked bodies

## Answer Key

1-A, 2-B, 3-B, 4-C, 5-D, 6-C, 7-D, 8-B, 9-B, 10-D
