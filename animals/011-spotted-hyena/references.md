## Well-established sources (safe to name)

- Hans Kruuk, *The Spotted Hyena: A Study of Predation and Social Behaviour* — the foundational scholarly field study of spotted hyena predation, feeding, and social behaviour.
- The Mara Hyena Project (Masai Mara, Kenya) associated with Kay Holekamp and colleagues — a major long-term body of research on wild spotted hyena social rank, communication, and cognition. Named here in general terms; specific findings should be traced to their individual publications and verified before final publication.
- IUCN Red List — species assessment for *Crocuta crocuta* (global status: Least Concern). Consult the current Red List entry for range, population trend, and threats.
- Animal Diversity Web (University of Michigan Museum of Zoology) — species profile for *Crocuta crocuta*.
- Standard animal-behavior textbooks (e.g. Alcock, *Animal Behavior*) for general ethological concepts such as cooperation, communication, dominance hierarchies, and the social-intelligence hypothesis.
