# Glossary — The Spotted Hyena (*Crocuta crocuta*)

**Clan** — the stable social group of a spotted hyena, ranging from a few individuals to more than a hundred, structured by a linear dominance hierarchy.
**Matriarchy** — a social system in which females are dominant; in spotted hyenas, adult females outrank adult males and females are on average larger.
**Fission-fusion** — a social pattern in which a stable group splits into smaller subgroups that forage and travel separately, then merge and separate again.
**Rank inheritance** — the process by which a cub acquires a social position just below its mother's, passing status down the female line across generations.
**Pseudo-penis** — the greatly enlarged clitoris of the female spotted hyena, through which she urinates, mates, and gives birth; a product of prenatal androgen exposure, not true hermaphroditism.
**Feliform** — a member of Feliformia, the cat-like carnivorans; hyenas are feliforms, more closely related to cats and mongooses than to dogs.
**Bone-cracking** — the ability, supported by powerful jaws and robust teeth, to break open bone to reach marrow, a food source most predators cannot exploit.
**Kleptoparasitism** — the stealing of food from another animal; hyenas and lions rob one another of kills in both directions.
**Latrine** — a communal defecation site used repeatedly by clan members as a scent-marking and information-sharing station.
**Pasting** — the deposition of a strong-smelling anal-gland secretion ("paste") onto grass stalks to mark trails and territory and to signal identity and status.
**Whoop** — the spotted hyena's long-distance, individually distinctive contact call, used to locate and recruit companions across a fission-fusion society.
**Cooperative hunting** — the coordinated pursuit of prey by two or more hyenas, allowing the capture of large ungulates that a single hyena could not take alone.
**Giggle (laugh)** — the staccato high-pitched call given under excitement or social stress, typically by a subordinate animal; not an expression of happiness.
**Greeting ceremony** — the ritual in which two hyenas stand head-to-tail and inspect each other's erect genitalia, reinforcing social bonds.
