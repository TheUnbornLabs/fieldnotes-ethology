# Quiz — The Humpback Whale

1. The humpback whale is best classified as:
   A. A baleen (filter-feeding) whale
   B. A toothed whale that echolocates
   C. A large fish
   D. A type of dolphin

2. The scientific name of the humpback whale is:
   A. *Orcinus orca*
   B. *Balaenoptera musculus*
   C. *Megaptera novaeangliae*
   D. *Physeter macrocephalus*

3. Humpback song is produced mainly by:
   A. Females on feeding grounds
   B. Calves
   C. Males, especially on breeding grounds
   D. Both sexes equally year-round

4. A song "revolution" refers to:
   A. A whale learning to echolocate
   B. A rapid, population-wide change in song type that can spread between populations
   C. Whales stopping singing entirely
   D. A change in migration route

5. Bubble-net feeding is:
   A. A method of echolocating prey
   B. A form of long-distance communication
   C. A courtship display
   D. A technique using rings of bubbles to concentrate prey before lunging

6. Lobtail feeding in the Gulf of Maine is notable because it:
   A. Proves humpbacks use tools
   B. Appears to have spread through the population by social learning
   C. Is used only by females
   D. Replaced bubble-net feeding entirely

7. Humpback social associations are best described as:
   A. Stable, lifelong pods
   B. Rigid matrilines
   C. Fluid and short-term, with the mother-calf bond as the main lasting tie
   D. Strictly solitary at all times

8. The leading hypothesis for the function of humpback song is that it serves in:
   A. Echolocation
   B. Navigation only
   C. Sexual selection, though the exact function is still debated
   D. Digestion

9. When humpbacks disrupt orca attacks on other species, the scientifically cautious interpretation is that:
   A. It proves humpbacks have human-like morality
   B. It may be a generalized anti-predator response, with the true motive unproven
   C. It never actually happens
   D. It is a hunting strategy

10. The current global IUCN Red List status of the humpback whale is:
    A. Extinct
    B. Critically Endangered
    C. Data Deficient
    D. Least Concern, reflecting broad recovery after commercial whaling

## Answer Key

1. A
2. C
3. C
4. B
5. D
6. B
7. C
8. C
9. B
10. D
