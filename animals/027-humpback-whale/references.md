## Verified (checked 2026-07-02)

- Scientific name *Megaptera novaeangliae*; baleen whale (Mysticeti), rorqual family Balaenopteridae — confirmed.
- IUCN Red List status Least Concern at the global species level — confirmed current. The species was down-listed from Vulnerable to Least Concern in 2008 following recovery from commercial whaling, and remains LC (2018 assessment, ~135,000 individuals, increasing trend). Some subpopulations remain listed at elevated risk (Vulnerable/Endangered) — consistent with the book's statement. Sources: IUCN "Humpback whale on road to recovery"; iucnredlist.org species 13006.
