- Mark L. Winston, *The Biology of the Honey Bee* — standard reference on honeybee biology and behavior.

## Verified (checked 2026-07-02)

- **Scientific name / taxonomy:** *Apis mellifera* (Western/European honeybee), order Hymenoptera, family Apidae — confirmed. Book's stated scope (this one species, while acknowledging other *Apis*) is appropriate.
- **Conservation:** As a managed species not globally endangered; wild subspecies of concern and colonies pressured by *Varroa*, disease, and pesticides — consistent with current understanding. (No single global IUCN "threatened" category for the managed species — correct.)
- **Waggle dance:** Confirmed well established. A forager's figure-eight dance encodes resource direction (angle relative to gravity/sun) and distance (waggle-run duration); decoded by Karl von Frisch (Nobel Prize 1973). Book text correct; von Frisch's book title cited in this file is genuine.
