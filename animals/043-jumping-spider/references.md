## Verified (checked 2026-07-02)

- Family **Salticidae** is the largest spider family, with well over 6,000 described species in 600+ genera (World Spider Catalog; consistent with published counts) — confirmed.
- Genus ***Maratus*** (peacock spiders): roughly 100 described species, endemic to Australia; more than 80 described 2011–2024 — confirmed (peacockspider.org; Wikipedia/Maratus; Museums Victoria).
- Genus ***Portia***: about twenty described species (older sources give ~17–20). The book's original "around a dozen" was an undercount and has been revised to "roughly twenty" (Wikipedia/Portia; ZooKeys 2021 revision).
- *Bagheera kiplingi* as the largely herbivorous salticid — confirmed as a well-documented example.
