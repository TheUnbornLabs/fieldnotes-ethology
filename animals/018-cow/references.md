## Verified (checked 2026-07-02)

The following were checked against the live IUCN Red List and Animal Diversity Web:

- Cattle, *Bos taurus* (order Artiodactyla, family Bovidae, subfamily Bovinae, genus *Bos*), with taurine (*B. t. taurus*) and indicine/zebu (*B. t. indicus*, sometimes *Bos indicus*) lineages — confirmed as an accepted taxonomy. Source: Animal Diversity Web.
