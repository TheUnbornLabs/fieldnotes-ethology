## Verified (checked 2026-07-02)

- Accepted scientific name ***Osphranter rufus*** (formerly *Macropus rufus*) — confirmed as the currently accepted genus (Mammal Diversity Database, mammaldiversity.org; Australian Government SPRAT; IUCN).
- Classification: Mammalia; Marsupialia; order Diprotodontia; family Macropodidae — confirmed.
- Largest living marsupial / largest native Australian land mammal, widespread across arid interior mainland Australia — confirmed.
- **IUCN Red List: Least Concern** — confirmed; abundant and widespread (tens of millions), populations fluctuate with rainfall, managed via regulated harvest.
