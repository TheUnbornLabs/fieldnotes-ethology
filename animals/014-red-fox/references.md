## Well-established sources (safe to name)

- David Macdonald, *Running with the Fox* — a well-known book on red fox behaviour and ecology by a leading carnivore biologist; a standard popular-science reference on the species.
- The Russian farm-fox experiment associated with Dmitry Belyaev and, subsequently, Lyudmila Trut — the long-running selective-breeding study of silver foxes for tameness and its insights into domestication syndrome. Named generally here; verify specific publications and dates before final publication.
- IUCN Red List — species assessment for *Vulpes vulpes* (global status: Least Concern). Consult the current Red List entry for range, population, and threats.

- Standard animal-behavior textbooks (e.g. Alcock, *Animal Behavior*) for general ethological concepts such as foraging, caching, communication, and social structure.
