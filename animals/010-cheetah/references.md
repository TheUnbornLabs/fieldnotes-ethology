## Verified (checked 2026-07-02)

The following were checked against the live web on 2026-07-02 and confirmed:

- **Scientific name & taxonomy:** *Acinonyx jubatus*, sole living member of genus *Acinonyx*, family Felidae, order Carnivora — confirmed (IUCN Red List).
- **IUCN Red List status:** *Acinonyx jubatus* assessed as **Vulnerable** (most recent assessment 2021; global population tentatively ~6,500 mature individuals) — confirmed via IUCN Red List (species 219). The Asiatic subspecies *A. j. venaticus* (Iran) and Northwest African *A. j. hecki* are Critically Endangered, matching the book's mention of a "critically endangered Asiatic population surviving in Iran."
