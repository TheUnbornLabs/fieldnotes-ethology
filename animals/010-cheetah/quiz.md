# Short Quiz — Book 010: The Cheetah

1. What is the cheetah's scientific name?
   A. *Panthera pardus*
   B. *Acinonyx jubatus*
   C. *Panthera leo*
   D. *Felis catus*

2. Roughly how long can a cheetah sustain a high-speed chase before overheating forces it to stop?
   A. About 20 to 30 seconds
   B. About 10 minutes
   C. About an hour
   D. Several hours

3. Which sound can a cheetah NOT make?
   A. Chirp
   B. Purr
   C. Roar
   D. Chuff

4. How are cheetah claws unusual among cats?
   A. They are fully retractable
   B. They are semi-retractable and grip like cleats
   C. They are absent
   D. They are venomous

5. What is the typical social pattern of male cheetahs?
   A. Large mixed prides
   B. Lifelong coalitions, often of brothers
   C. Always strictly solitary
   D. Female-led herds

6. Why are cheetahs mainly active by day?
   A. To photosynthesize
   B. To avoid nocturnal competitors like lions and hyenas
   C. Because they cannot see at night at all
   D. To stay warm

7. What term describes cheetahs losing kills to lions and hyenas?
   A. Symbiosis
   B. Kleptoparasitism
   C. Mutualism
   D. Hibernation

8. What is the IUCN Red List status of the cheetah?
   A. Least Concern
   B. Extinct
   C. Vulnerable
   D. Domesticated

9. The cheetah's "genetic bottleneck" refers to:
   A. A narrow windpipe
   B. Low genetic diversity from a past population crash
   C. A hunting technique
   D. A type of coat pattern

10. Which is a likely function of the cheetah's tear marks?
    A. Storing water
    B. Reducing glare and aiding visual focus
    C. Producing venom
    D. Attracting prey with light

## Answer Key

1. B
2. A
3. C
4. B
5. B
6. B
7. B
8. C
9. B
10. B
