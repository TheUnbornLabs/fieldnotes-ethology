# Book 003 — The Chimpanzee: Quiz

1. To which group do chimpanzees belong?
A. Monkeys
B. Great apes
C. Lemurs
D. Lesser apes

2. What term describes chimpanzee societies splitting into changing subgroups?
A. Eusociality
B. Fission-fusion
C. Solitary living
D. Colonial nesting

3. In chimpanzee communities, which sex typically disperses at maturity?
A. Males
B. Both equally
C. Females
D. Neither

4. The pant-hoot is primarily a form of:
A. Tool use
B. Vocal communication
C. Grooming
D. Nest building

5. Which technique is used to extract termites?
A. Hammer-and-anvil
B. Buttress drumming
C. Termite-fishing
D. Consortship

6. At which site were chimpanzees documented making sharpened sticks to hunt bushbabies?
A. Gombe
B. Taï
C. Fongoli
D. Mahale

7. Which researcher's Gombe study first reported chimpanzee tool use?
A. Frans de Waal
B. Jane Goodall
C. Dian Fossey
D. Andrew Whiten

8. Compared with chimpanzees, bonobo societies are generally:
A. More male-dominated and more aggressive
B. Less influenced by females
C. More influenced by females and less aggressive
D. Identical in structure

9. What is the IUCN Red List status of *Pan troglodytes*?
A. Least Concern
B. Vulnerable
C. Endangered
D. Extinct in the Wild

10. The claim that chimpanzees are "five to ten times stronger" than humans is:
A. Precisely correct
B. Exaggerated; the real advantage is more modest
C. An underestimate
D. Impossible to measure

## Answer Key

1. B
2. B
3. C
4. B
5. C
6. C
7. B
8. C
9. C
10. B
