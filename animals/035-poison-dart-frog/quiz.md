# Short Quiz — The Poison Dart Frog (Family Dendrobatidae)

1. The toxicity of poison dart frogs is:
   A. Sequestered from their arthropod diet
   B. Manufactured internally from birth
   C. Absorbed from rainwater
   D. Present only in captive-bred frogs

2. Aposematism refers to:
   A. Advertising a real defence with a conspicuous warning signal
   B. Hiding through camouflage
   C. Mimicking a plant
   D. Storing food for later

3. Most poison dart frogs are unusual among frogs in being:
   A. Strictly nocturnal
   B. Entirely aquatic
   C. Diurnal, active by day
   D. Wingless gliders

4. Captive-bred poison dart frogs raised on fruit flies and crickets are:
   A. More toxic than wild ones
   B. Unable to survive
   C. Identical in toxicity to wild ones
   D. Essentially non-toxic

5. In *Oophaga pumilio*, the mother provisions each tadpole with:
   A. Live insects
   B. Regurgitated fish
   C. Unfertilised "nurse" eggs
   D. Nothing; tadpoles feed themselves

6. Tadpole transport in dendrobatids means:
   A. Tadpoles swim to pools on their own
   B. Eggs are laid directly in ponds
   C. Tadpoles are carried by wind
   D. A parent carries tadpoles on its back to small water bodies

7. The most toxic dendrobatid, among the most toxic vertebrates known, is:
   A. *Oophaga pumilio*
   B. *Dendrobates tinctorius*
   C. *Phyllobates terribilis*
   D. *Bufo bufo*

8. Müllerian mimicry involves:
   A. A harmless species copying a defended one
   B. Two or more defended species converging on a shared warning signal
   C. A frog imitating a leaf
   D. Camouflage against bark

9. Which is the correct description of dart frogs' defence?
   A. They are poisonous, storing sequestered toxins in skin glands
   B. They are venomous and inject toxin
   C. They spray acid at predators
   D. They have no chemical defence

10. A major conservation threat to poison dart frogs is:
    A. Competition from bats
    B. Overabundance of ants
    C. Excessive rainfall only
    D. The chytrid fungus, along with habitat loss and the pet trade

## Answer Key

1. A
2. A
3. C
4. D
5. C
6. D
7. C
8. B
9. A
10. D
