## Verified (checked 2026-07-02)

- Scientific name *Oryctolagus cuniculus*; sole living species of its genus; family Leporidae, order Lagomorpha — confirmed.
- IUCN Red List status: CORRECTED. The species was reassessed as **Endangered (EN)** in 2019 (assessors Villafuerte & Delibes-Mateos), up-listed from Near Threatened, following an approximately 70% global decline driven by rabbit haemorrhagic disease (RHDV2) in the native Iberian range. The book, summary, quiz, and references previously stated "Near Threatened"; all occurrences were corrected to Endangered. Sources: IUCN Red List (species 41291); PMC12466530; Springer "European Rabbit" (2023).
