## Verified (checked 2026-07-02)

- "Squid" = cephalopods of the order **Teuthida** (class Cephalopoda, phylum Mollusca) — confirmed.
- Humboldt/jumbo squid ***Dosidicus gigas***, eastern Pacific (Chile to California), large pelagic predator — confirmed (NOAA; ADW).
- Market squid ***Doryteuthis opalescens***, **formerly *Loligo opalescens*** (name changed following the 2003 CIAC symposium consensus); eastern Pacific, British Columbia to Baja — confirmed (NOAA Fisheries; California Dept. of Fish & Wildlife; ADW).
- Both are heavily fished but productive, fast-turnover semelparous stocks rather than classically endangered species; status is species-specific and data-limited. (Group with two named species — no single IUCN threatened category applies.)
