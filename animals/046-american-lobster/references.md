## Verified (checked 2026-07-02)

- Scientific name ***Homarus americanus***; family **Nephropidae** (clawed lobsters); order Decapoda, class Malacostraca; closest relative *Homarus gammarus* — confirmed.
- NW Atlantic range (Labrador/Newfoundland south to North Carolina; greatest abundance Gulf of Maine) — confirmed (NOAA Fisheries; Wikipedia).
- Long lifespan with the largest individuals estimated near a century, growth continuing through life via molting — confirmed (AnAge/senescence.info gives ~100 years maximum longevity; Natural History Museum).
