# Species Master List — 100 Ethology Books

The confirmed 100-species list. Numbering is permanent and drives folder names and website ordering. Slugs are the folder names. Scientific names are provisional labels for the author's convenience and must be re-checked against an authority (ITIS, GBIF, or IUCN) at final QC.

The starting list was reviewed for taxonomic and ecological spread and retained with only minor confirmation. It already covers the required diversity, so no substitutions were needed for Batch 1.

## Notes on selection

The list balances celebrated species (wolf, lion, dolphin, raven, peregrine) against under-appreciated ones (fiddler crab, axolotl, three-toed sloth, village weaver, nightjar), and pairs each with a clear behavioral teaching hook to prevent overlap between books. Several species classic to the history of ethology are included deliberately: the greylag goose (Lorenz's imprinting work), the herring gull (Tinbergen's releaser studies), and the honeybee (von Frisch's waggle dance).
